Flash on S31

When erasing or writing to SPI flash, by default the contents on the flash cannot be read simutaneously.

Fortunately, for the specific SPI flash model on the S31 module, it supports auto-suspend, which allows reads to interrupt writing.

Since erasing and writing to flash requires more complex operations than mapping to MMU, a flash proxy helper is added in OpenSBI to run the ROM-provided flash functions in M-mode.
